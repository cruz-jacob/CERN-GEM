Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I integrate Vigilant Shield into my game?

Integration is designed to be straightforward. We provide comprehensive SDKs for popular game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine, along with detailed documentation. You can find the guides in our Developer Portal or contact our support team for assistance.

What is the performance impact of Vigilant Shield?

We've engineered Vigilant Shield to have minimal performance impact on both client devices and game servers. Our processes are highly optimized to ensure a seamless experience for your players.

Technical & Advanced Topics

How does Vigilant Shield handle new and unknown cheats?

Our system uses a combination of heuristic analysis, machine learning, and behavioral detection to identify suspicious activities that may indicate new or unknown cheats. We also have a dedicated research team constantly updating our threat intelligence.

What types of games is Vigilant Shield suitable for?

Vigilant Shield is adaptable to a wide range of online multiplayer games, from first-person shooters and MOBAs to MMORPGs and competitive strategy games. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How are false positives handled?

We take false positives very seriously. Our detection algorithms are rigorously tested, and we have systems in place for reviewing and rapidly addressing any suspected false positives. We also provide tools for developers to manage and report such instances.
