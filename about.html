Our Story

From a passionate idea to a global leader in game security, our journey has been driven by a single purpose: to ensure fair play for everyone.

2023 - The Genesis

Vigilant Shield was founded by a group of veteran cybersecurity experts and passionate gamers who saw the growing threat of cheating in online multiplayer games.

2024 - First Breakthrough

Launched our first AI-powered detection engine, revolutionizing the industry by identifying complex cheats with unprecedented accuracy and minimal performance impact.

2025 - Protecting Millions

Today, Vigilant Shield protects over 50 million players across dozens of titles, partnering with studios worldwide to build a more secure and fair gaming ecosystem.
